## To integrate PreCallAI with Stripe, follow these steps:

<ul>

    <li>Navigate to the integration option within PreCallAI and select Stripe.</li>

    <li>Log in using your Stripe email address and password.</li>

    <li>Verify the authentication prompt sent by Stripe to ensure secure access.</li>

    <li>Once logged in, you will be redirected back to the PreCallAI dashboard.</li>

    <li>Choose the Stripe account you wish to connect with your PreCallAI account to confirm a successful connection to Stripe.</li>

    <li>Go to the Billing & Payments option under the Assistant Settings section.</li>

    <li>Open the payment workflow where you want Stripe to be activated.</li>

    <li>Click on the Enable Payment Gateway option and select Stripe from the list.</li>

    <li>Navigate to the Payment Actions tab to define when payments should be triggered.</li>

    <li>Set up your preferred actions like sending a payment link, collecting payment after a call, or auto-generating invoices.</li>

    <li>Provide a short description for internal reference and save the configuration.</li>

    <li>Test the Stripe payment flow by simulating a customer call using the PreCallAI dashboard.</li>

</ul>

Ensure that the payment event is logged successfully under the Call Summary section.

Finally, save all changes in the Assistant Settings to complete the integration process with Stripe.
