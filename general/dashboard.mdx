---
title: Dashboard Feature
---

The new Dashboard feature allows users to view key metrics at a glance:

Total Audience: Displays the total number of users.

Total Minutes: Shows the total calling time.

Total Calls Done: Indicates the total number of calls completed.

Additionally, users can access the latest call recordings and campaign lists. By clicking on "See All," users will be redirected to the call logs and Campaign for more detailed information.
