## What is a <span style={{color:"#ca8a04"}}>Contact List</span> ?

We essentially group a bunch of contacts and form a contact list. Contact list is just a group of users that we intend to target.

## How to add a <span style={{color:"#ca8a04"}}>Contact List</span> ?

Follow these steps to add a contact list:<br/>
Login into the [dashboard](https://app.precallai.com/) and open [Contact List](https://app.precallai.com/admin/contact-list) section.<br/>
Then:

<span style={{ fontWeight: 800 }}>Step 1 :</span> Click on the <span
  style={{ color: "#ca8a04" }}>
  First Contact List
</span> button <br />
<span style={{ fontWeight: 800 }}>Step 2 :</span> Add Name and description <br />
<span style={{ fontWeight: 800 }}>Step 3 :</span> Cick on <span
  style={{ color: "#ca8a04" }}>
  Save
</span> button
